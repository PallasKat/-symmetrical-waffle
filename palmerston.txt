Palmerston is the resident Chief Mouser of the Foreign & Commonwealth Office 
(FCO) at Whitehall, London. He is a two-year-old, black and white, bicolor cat, 
who began his role and the position of Chief Mouser on 13 April 2016. 
Previously, he was from Battersea Dogs & Cats Home and is named after the former 
Foreign Minister and Prime Minister Lord Palmerston. He is employed at the King 
Charles Street building.

The inception of the position of Chief Mouser at the FCO followed from the 
position Chief Mouser of 10 Downing Street, Larry and from visits from George 
Osborne's cat Freya who made regular visits to the office.
